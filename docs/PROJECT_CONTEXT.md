AI Growth OS — Project Context
Project Name
AI Growth OS
Founder
Halim Belkacem
Primary Product
ForgCV (https://forgcv.com)
AI Growth OS is being built initially to operate the complete growth strategy of ForgCV. The architecture must never contain hardcoded ForgCV logic. ForgCV is simply the first workspace using the platform.
Vision
AI Growth OS is an intelligent Growth Operating System.
Its mission is to centralize every important growth signal in one place, transform those signals into actionable recommendations, help the founder prioritize work, generate high-quality drafts, measure results, and continuously improve through learning.
The system must dramatically reduce the amount of manual work required to grow an online business.
The founder should no longer need to manually inspect dozens of websites, dashboards or social platforms every day.
Instead, AI Growth OS continuously gathers information, analyzes it, and proposes the highest-impact actions.
Long-Term Goal
Become the operating system for company growth.
Not another analytics dashboard.
Not another AI writer.
Not another SEO tool.
Instead, a system that understands business growth end-to-end and helps founders make better decisions every day.
Core Philosophy
Action First.
Every feature must reduce decision effort.
The product exists to answer:
“What is the most important thing I should do next to grow my business?”
Human Control
The system never publishes content automatically.
The system never sends emails automatically.
The system never modifies external services automatically.
Every external action requires explicit human approval.
AI prepares.
The founder decides.
Primary Capabilities
The platform will eventually:

* Monitor website traffic.
* Monitor SEO.
* Monitor Google Search Console.
* Monitor Google Analytics.
* Monitor competitors.
* Monitor AI industry news.
* Monitor social platforms.
* Monitor community discussions.
* Detect opportunities.
* Recommend actions.
* Generate drafts.
* Measure outcomes.
* Learn from previous results.
First Workspace
The first workspace is ForgCV.
ForgCV serves as the real-world validation environment for AI Growth OS.
No architecture component may contain ForgCV-specific logic.
The platform must support multiple workspaces in the future.
Current Status
Architecture: Frozen (v2.1)
Implementation: Not started
Current objective:
Complete Phase 0 (foundation) before implementing any business features.
All implementation must follow the frozen architecture documents located inside /docs.
No architectural changes may be introduced without following the official Decision Lifecycle.
